Gameplay Features

In Capybara Clicker Pro, you start by clicking on the main capybara to earn "Capy Coins". Use these coins to adopt more capybaras, who will automatically generate coins for you. As you progress, you'll unlock:

Upgrades: Boost your capybaras' coin production with fun items like tiny hats, snacks, and toys.

Habitats: Expand your paradise with different environments like tropical beaches, bamboo forests, and cozy burrows.

Special Events: Participate in seasonal events to collect limited-edition capybaras and decorations.

Capybara Fun Facts

Excellent Swimmers They can stay underwater for up to 5 minutes
Social Animals Live in groups of 10-20 individuals
Herbivores Diet consists mainly of grasses and aquatic plants

Pro Tips for Capybara Masters

Become a capybara whisperer with these expert strategies:

1. Balanced Investment: Don't spend all your coins on new capybaras. Invest in upgrades that boost your entire colony's production.

2. Prestige Strategy: When you reset your progress with the "Chill Out" feature, you'll earn special bonuses that help you progress faster.

3. Habitat Synergy: Combine capybaras and decorations from the same habitat for bonus production.

4. Active vs. Idle: Play actively for quick boosts, but set up your colony for efficient idle production when you're away.

5. Daily Rewards: Log in every day to collect special bonuses and rare capybaras!
