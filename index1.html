About Sky Riders BUK

Sky Riders BUK is an exhilarating motorcycle racing game that takes place high in the sky. Race through floating tracks, perform incredible stunts, and compete against other riders in this action-packed arcade game.

With its unique sky-based racing mechanics and stunning visuals, Sky Riders BUK offers an exciting gaming experience that combines racing, stunts, and strategy. Master the controls, collect power-ups, and become the ultimate sky rider!

Game Controls

Keyboard Controls

  • Arrow Keys: Control motorcycle
  • Space: Perform stunts
  • Shift: Boost

Touch Controls

  • Swipe: Control direction
  • Tap: Perform stunts
  • Double Tap: Boost
